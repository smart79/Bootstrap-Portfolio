About Me

Chris brings over 15 years of Executive and Operational Management and 12 years of Sales and Marketing Management experience. After serving 5 years in the United States Marines, he went on to earn an Associates Degree in Marketing from Odessa College. Over the past 15 years Chris has enjoyed a successful career as Sales and Marketing Director with two successful Financial Services startups, Sales and Marketing Manager for 2 fortune 500 companies, including one of the largest Search Engine Optimization Companies in Austin.

After graduating from The Full Stack Flex Web Developer program at Southern Methodist University in Dallas Texas, Chris' passions for Web Technologies and Internet Marketing combined with his entrepreneurial spirit and Expert Sales and Marketing experience lead him to seek out a career in Web Devlopment that would combine these strengths of his. As a coding zealot, Chris continues to hone his coding skills everyday while in search for fulltime or paid internship employment.
